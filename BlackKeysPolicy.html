Black Keys privacy policy


This is the privacy policy for Black Keys, custom keyboard for iOS. This policy exists to inform you about security when using Black Keys

We do not receive any personal information about you or any of the text that you enter, nor do we log any passwords or credit card details.

You may encounter an alert when installing our application on your iOS device. This alert appears whenever a third-party keyboard is enabled and warns that keyboards “may” collect or log sensitive data (passwords, credit card information, etc.)

We do not support typing in pasword field in your languages, it's normal when some security field automatic switch back to apple system keyboard.

You will see the text "Not Verify" when install custom font, but the font not damage your device.
